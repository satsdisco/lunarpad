What

A platform for HTML presentations. Upload any deck — reveal.js, Slidev, Marp, or raw HTML — and share it with your team.
Built for hackathons, demo days, and teams that build in public.
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Features

  • Upload — drag & drop .html or .zip. auto-thumbnails. sandboxed viewer.
  • Present — fullscreen mode. embed codes. shareable links.
  • Build in Public — bounties, project showcase, speaker signups.
  • Demo Days — create events, manage lineups, live voting.
  • Vote — audience upvotes. leaderboards. prize pools.
